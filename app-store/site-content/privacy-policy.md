# What If Invest Privacy Policy

What If Invest is designed as a local historical investing simulator.

## Data Stored On Device

Saved scenarios are stored locally on your device so you can reopen them later.

## Network Access

The app may refresh historical market data from a public finance data source. The request is used to update historical price data for simulations.

## Accounts And Tracking

The app does not require an account for the core simulator experience. It does not include third-party advertising or cross-app tracking in the current release.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact KinnoLabs through the support channel listed on the App Store product page.
