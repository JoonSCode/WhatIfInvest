# WhatIfInvest Specification

## Goal

Help a beginner investor feel "if I had invested then, what would it be worth now?" within roughly 15 seconds.

## v1 Product Rules

- Start from a prefilled scenario.
- Show a single scenario before asking for comparison.
- Support US major ETFs and Magnificent 7.
- Use adjusted close for hindsight calculations.
- Keep basic save and basic share free.
- If ads exist, keep them below the fold on the result screen only.
